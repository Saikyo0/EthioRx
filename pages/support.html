Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I place an order?

    Visit the Shop, select your medication, add to cart, and proceed to checkout.

  • How can I track my order?

    Check your email for tracking details or visit your user dashboard.

  • What should I do if I don't receive my order confirmation?

    Check your spam folder, and contact our support if you still can't find it.

  • Can I cancel or modify my order?

    Yes, within 30 minutes of placing it. Contact our support team for assistance.

  • What if I have a prescription from a doctor?

    You can upload your prescription during the ordering process.
